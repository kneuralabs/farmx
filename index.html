Switching roles

Use the pill switcher at the top right to move between the three dashboards. Everything is connected: an action in one role updates the others live. Try requesting a booking as an Enterprise, then open the Vendor and Admin tabs to see it appear.

A note on editing

Every form is labelled with a You are the … tag so you always know who does what. Any record with a ✎ or ✕ button can be edited or removed on the spot — changes flow to every screen, because they all read from the same shared records.
